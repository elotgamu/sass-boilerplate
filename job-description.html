Job Description

Senior Director – Import and Trade Compliance

?Other locations/Remote Available

ABOUT THE OPPORTUNITY:

TN Name is looking for a Senior Director with 7+ years of experience to join its fast-growing Trade Compliance group. Ideal candidates will have experience in US Imports, US Free Trade Agreements, and World Trade Organization matters. Must be an expert in US import laws and regulations. Ankura provides the opportunity to work collaboratively on small, diverse teams with skilled, intelligent, driven and successful individuals. Ankura is focused on hiring people that will serve as the foundation of a growing firm dedicated to its people, culture and delivering high quality service to our clients. Up to 50% travel required.

ESSENTIAL DUTIES & RESPONSIBILITIES:

As a Senior Director, you will be a key member of the engagement team. Responsibilities will include, but will not be limited to, the following:


• Advise clients on US import and trade matters, including compliance program design and execution, product classification, duties, tariffs, etc. .


• Lead client engagement teams.


• Design, conduct, and support import and trade audits.


• Conduct and support import/trade-related corporate investigations.


• Engage government agencies and industry groups on behalf of clients.


• Thought leader (i.e., publishing articles and delivering presentations/webinars) on current/pressing import and trade issues.


• Identify and develop potential business opportunities and clients in the import/trade space.

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS, SKILLS & CHARACTERISTICS:

  • Bachelor’s Degree required, Master’s Degree in a related field a plus.
  • Prior US CBP, WTO employment strongly preferred
  • Anti- dumping and countervailing duties experience a plus
  • Audit experience a major plus
  • Technology development/design experience a plus
  • Must possess strong quantitative analytical skills, proficiency in MS Excel; Database proficiency a plus.

  • Must be a self- starter and able to operate independently.
  • Possess strong team-oriented personality characteristics: hard-working, thoughtful, respectful, and collaborative, with a positive outlook and attitude.
  • Able to demonstrate excellent communication (written and verbal), quantitative analysis, and organizational skills.
  • Highly motivated, eager to learn, willing to travel, and able to motivate self and others
  • Able to mentor and develop junior colleagues
  • Flexibility to travel as required

ABOUT TN Name

TN Name, LLC (“Ankura”) is a business advisory and expert services firm with locations nationwide. Its deep understanding of the opportunities and challenges clients face enables its team to provide impactful, senior-level counsel. As an independent firm built on five key principles – Integrity, Quality, Diversity, Collaboration and Longevity – Ankura's relationships extend beyond one engagement or issue. The firm empowers its industry experts to provide a high-touch, unique approach for its clients in critical times. Ankura's offering includes risk, resilience & geopolitical, investigations & forensic accounting, litigation & disputes, turnaround & restructuring, and regulatory & contractual compliance.
